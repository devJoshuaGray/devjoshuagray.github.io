Joshua Gray

Software Developer

"I am a passionate professional seeking to make an impact on culture and stay up to date on the latest technologies to better serve mankind."

I am a software developer with experience in Front End , as well as Back End Languages and Frameworks. I am a Bethel School of Technology graduate who is highly motivated to be a lifelong learner. In my previous career I worked for over a decade in the aviation field as a structural and electrical aircraft processor. Since 2015, I have been serving as a worship leader at my local church. I have learned how to keep schedule and manage my time effectively, as well as how to overcome challenges and be flexible. I have always had a passion for software and problem solving. I also enjoy Agile Developement practices and think I would be an excellent fit in that type of environment.

Throughout my journey of life I have been relatively content with how things were. I thought if I had a good job and could provide for my family then I could stay happy. This of course is not a bad thing, but I could feel more on my life. I had a few defining moments that I knew would shape how I approach my future. I began to discover a burning desire to make a difference in the tech space, to give myself over to that journey of discovery, and actually enjoy the process. I truly love working in this industry and everything it has to offer. I am not naive in thinking that I will always enjoy every aspect, but I do believe that even through the difficulties I can have a positive attitude and soak up knowledge through the process. This frees me to learn and grow from my mistakes. I believe I can thrive in those sometimes difficult hours of problem solving, and offer valuable solutions to contribute to an affective team.

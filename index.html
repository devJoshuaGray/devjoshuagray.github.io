Project Spotlight

GOOD NEWS STATION

This application is a passion project between myself and four other gentlemen at Bethel School of Technology. For this project, we decided there was a constant stream of bad news present on the internet but not nearly enough places focusing on the good and inspiring news. Thus, Good News Station was born. The intent was to have a social site similar to that of Twitter or Reddit that allowed others to go to and share their own inspirational and good news stories. This platform would also be a place that is uplifting and inspiring without all the hate and negativity that comes with a constant pursuit of ratings. Here, the good news and generous acts, that all too often get buried or forgotten in the clutter of negative headlines get their moment to shine and can help inspire others to "Not become weary in doing good."

-TOOLS USED-

ReactJS / HTML / CSS / MongoDB / Mongoose / Express / Nodejs
